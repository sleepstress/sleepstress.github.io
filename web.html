STRESS & SLEEP
ABOUT OUR PROJECT

Due to some research on mental health, we found that sleep quality and stress play important roles in daily life. In this paper, we determined a correlation between variability in sleep duration and quality and stress levels during the day. We took 5 subjects and assess their sleep quality by asking some questions related to 7 components, such as subjective sleep quality, sleep duration and so on.

In addition, the participant will answer the question of μEMA.Considering the long-term goal that detecting stress without answer question once an hour, we use the mean of heart rate measure stress level. And the survey they filled out contained information about deadline related stress in order to avoid a confounding variable.

OUR STUDY PROCEDURE

Here is our system structure:

Long-term goal: We wants to design an app which can detect stress in advance based on heart rate change so that the user do not need to answer wether they are stressed or not once an hour, which is very annoying.

Short-term goal: We find several participant to help us answer the question wether they are stressed once an hour, at the same time, wear the E4 sensor. We build an offline model to find the relationship between heart rate and stress level.

Data Collection: We have three participants to answer our questionnaire about sleep quality assenment. Each person required to answer for two days.

The figure above showed selected questions in the sleep survey. We can see that all the sleep scores for each two days are over 4 which means their sleep quality are bad. The score is calculated based on the e Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index(PSQI).

This graph indicates physical data collected by E4 sensor. Here shows the variation digram of temperature, Heart Rate and Acceleremoters. The sensor also can detect EDA and Blood Volume Pulse. In our project, we only focus on the relationship between the varation of Heart Rate and stress level.

DEMO & RESULT

Based on the data of our participants, we found that students who has good sleep quality are less possible to feel stress. And the hearte rate will go higher and change frequently if the student are stressed. The more hey feel stressed, the higher their heart rate are.
